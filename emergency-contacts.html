Medical Emergency

  • 1 Stay calm and assess the situation
  • 2 Call emergency services immediately
  • 3 Follow dispatcher instructions

Fire Emergency

  • 1 Evacuate the building immediately
  • 2 Call fire emergency services
  • 3 Don't use elevators
  • 4 Meet at designated assembly point

  • 1 Ensure your safety first
  • 2 Call emergency services
  • 3 Set up warning triangles
  • 4 Document the scene if possible
